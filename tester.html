About us

Tatar High School was founded in 1942 by Azamat Al-Casis, who sooner became its' first principal. Over 70 years our school has been providing quality education that helped our students to achieve their goals in the future. The mission of Tatar High is to create all the conditions in order to unlock the potential of each student and prepare him for the future.
